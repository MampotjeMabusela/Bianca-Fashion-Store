4. Deposits & Payment

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your order and commence work. The deposit amount will be stated on your quotation, typically between 40% and 60% of the total order value.

The balance is due on or before collection of your garment, unless another payment schedule is agreed in writing. We accept payment methods as communicated at the time of order (e.g. bank transfer, cash, or other agreed methods).

Late payment may delay collection or release of finished garments. Prices quoted are in South African Rand (ZAR) unless stated otherwise.
