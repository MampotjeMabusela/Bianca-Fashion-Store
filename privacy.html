1. Introduction

Bianca Fashion Shop (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle data when you:

  • Visit our website (including pages hosted at bianca-fashion-store.vercel.app)
  • Contact us by email, WhatsApp, or our online contact form
  • Book a consultation or place a custom tailoring order

By using our website or services, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy. For terms governing orders and services, please see our Terms & Conditions.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Information you provide directly

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number, WhatsApp number)
  • Messages and enquiries submitted via our contact form, email, or WhatsApp
  • Body measurements, sizing, style preferences, and design choices for bespoke orders
  • Order details, quotations, payment references, and fitting appointment information
  • Photographs you share for design reference (only with your consent)

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data when you visit our website (e.g. browser type, device type, pages viewed)
  • IP address and general location (country/region level), typically via our hosting provider
  • Date and time of your visit

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as health data) unless you voluntarily provide it in relation to a fitting or garment requirement, and only where relevant to our service.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for legitimate business purposes, including to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Process orders, quotations, fittings, and bespoke tailoring services
  • Communicate with you about your order status, appointments, and collection
  • Improve our website, services, and client experience
  • Send service-related messages (we do not sell your data for marketing lists)
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

We will not use your information for purposes incompatible with those described above without notifying you.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under applicable data protection laws (including the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (“POPIA”) in South Africa), we process your data based on one or more of the following grounds:

  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide tailoring services you have requested
  • Consent: Where you have agreed (e.g. submitting a contact form or sharing photos)
  • Legitimate interests: To operate our business, prevent fraud, and improve our services
  • Legal obligation: Where we must retain or disclose information by law

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers who help us operate (e.g. email delivery, website hosting, form processing) under confidentiality obligations
  • Fabric suppliers or contractors only where necessary to fulfil your order (e.g. garment production), and limited to relevant details
  • Legal authorities if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety

Where data is transferred outside South Africa, we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

7. How Long We Keep Data

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Enquiries: Typically up to 24 months, unless an order follows
  • Client and order records: For the duration of our relationship and up to 7 years thereafter for accounting, tax, and legal purposes
  • Measurements: While you remain an active client, or until you request deletion
  • Website logs: As determined by our hosting provider, generally a short retention period

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymise it where possible.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. These include secure hosting, limited access to client records, and careful handling of communications.

No method of transmission over the internet (including email and WhatsApp) is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal and contractual limits)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa) if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at biancamandela297@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Cookies & Analytics

Our website is a static site and does not use invasive tracking cookies for advertising purposes. Our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect standard server logs and technical cookies necessary for security and performance.

If we add analytics or additional cookies in future, we will update this policy and, where required, seek your consent.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the website function.

11. Third-Party Services

Our website and communications may involve third-party services, including:

  • Vercel — website hosting and content delivery
  • Web3Forms — processing messages submitted through our contact form
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — if you contact us via WhatsApp, your messages are subject to WhatsApp’s own privacy policy
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded from Google servers when you visit our site
  • Instagram — links to our social profile; governed by Meta’s policies when you visit their platform

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services. We are not responsible for their practices outside our control.

12. Children

Our website and services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have received information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will indicate when changes were made.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
